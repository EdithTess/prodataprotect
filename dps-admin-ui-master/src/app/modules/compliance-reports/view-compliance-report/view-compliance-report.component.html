ORGANISATIONAL BACKGROUND

The Personal Data Protection Office (PDPO) was established to, among others, oversee the implementation of and be responsible for the enforcement of the Data Protection and Privacy Act and the Regulations thereunder. According to Regulation 50, every data collector, data processor and data controller registered under these Regulations shall submit within ninety (90) days after the end of each financial year, submit to the PDPO a summary of;

  1. All complaints received and the status of such complaints, including whether the complaint was resolved or is still pending.
  2. All data breaches and the actions taken to address them.

Regulation 4(b) of the Data Protection and Privacy Regulations mandates PDPO to coordinate, supervise, and monitor data collectors, data processors and data controllers. To this end, PDPO will monitor the said persons by expanding the scope of the annual compliance report to ensure the desired levels of compliance are reached.

The financial year of PDPO is the same as that of Government that runs from 1st July to 30th June as stipulated by section 30 of the National Information Technology Authority, Uganda (NITA-U) Act of 2009. In adherence to this requirement, {{orgName}} hereby submits its annual Data Protection and Privacy compliance report to PDPO.
